Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before we start working together.

Services

I help businesses make better decisions and run more efficiently using data, automation, and AI. This covers six areas: data analysis & reporting, interactive dashboards, business process automation, custom AI tools, market research, and strategic consulting. Think of it as a one-stop shop for anything data or strategy-related — without the overhead of a large firm.
I've worked across 15+ industries including B2B SaaS, EdTech, FinTech, D2C e-commerce, healthcare, legal, real estate, retail, and consulting firms. The underlying challenges — messy data, manual processes, unclear strategy — tend to be universal.

Pricing

Three models: Starter Sprint (₹15K for a single deliverable), Growth Project (₹45K for a full engagement), and Retainer (₹30K/month for ongoing advisory). These are starting rates — final pricing depends on scope and complexity. Enterprise scopes are negotiated separately.
Yes — the free 30-minute discovery call isn't just a sales call. I typically identify one or two quick wins during the session and share them freely. I may also do a small complimentary data audit if it helps scope the engagement properly.
Primarily by project — fixed-scope projects give both sides more clarity and prevent scope creep anxiety. For ongoing retainers, it's a monthly flat rate with an agreed hour allocation. Hourly billing is available for advisory-only engagements at ₹3,000–5,000/hour.

Process

Starter Sprints typically deliver in 5–7 days, Growth Projects in 2–3 weeks, and Retainer work is ongoing. Quick-turnaround options exist for urgent needs — a simple analysis or automation script can often be done in 48–72 hours if the scope is tight.
As involved as you want to be. I run a low-friction async process — most projects are managed over email or WhatsApp with 1–2 brief check-ins. I'll never block progress waiting for a meeting when a quick message will do.
All Growth Projects and Retainers include a 30-day post-delivery support window — if something breaks, needs tweaking, or you have questions, I'm available at no extra charge. Many clients then transition to a lighter retainer to keep things running.
Yes, always. I'm NDA-ready from day one and treat all client data, strategies, and business information as strictly confidential. I will never share your data with third parties or reference your business publicly without explicit written permission.

Tech & Tools

Core stack: Python (pandas, scikit-learn) for data work, SQL for database queries, Power BI / Tableau / Looker for dashboards, Zapier / Make for automation, and OpenAI / Claude / LangChain for AI tools. I match tools to your existing ecosystem wherever possible.

Results & Expectations

I guarantee the quality and completeness of my deliverables — on time, documented, and meeting the agreed spec. What I can't guarantee is specific business outcomes, because those depend on how the work is adopted on your side. I focus on building things with measurable impact.
For automation and dashboard projects, impact is often immediate — you'll save hours within the first week of deployment. For strategy and market research, impact is seen over weeks and months as decisions get made and executed.
Every project includes revision rounds — 1 for Starter Sprints, 3 for Growth Projects. If after revisions the deliverable still doesn't meet the agreed brief, we'll work it out together. Open, honest communication is the best way to prevent this — and I always flag issues early.
